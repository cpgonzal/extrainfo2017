# véase http://www.michael-noll.com/tutorials/running-hadoop-on-ubuntu-linux-multi-node-cluster/

-Edit in all machines the /etc/hosts file and add the master and slaves IP adresses

10.6.129.230 master
10.6.128.31  slave1
10.6.128.39  slave2
10.6.128.215 slave3
10.6.128.151 slave4


-(optional) change in all machines the hostname in:
/etc/hostname

hostnamectl set-hostname master
/etc/init.d/hostname.sh start

-Copy the public SSH key to the authorized_keys file of slaves:

ssh-copy-id -i $HOME/.ssh/id_rsa.pub bigdata@slave1
...

(no es necesario ya que el STIC ha clonado todas las máquinas a partir del máster)


-Update in master the $HADOOP_HOME/etc/hadoop/masters:

master


-Update in master the $HADOOP_HOME/etc/hadoop/slaves:

master
slave1
slave2
slave3
...


-Change in all machines the localhost by master in $HADOOP_HOME/etc/hadoop/core-site.xml:

scp $HADOOP_HOME/etc/hadoop/core-site.xml bigdata@slave1:$HADOOP_HOME/etc/hadoop
